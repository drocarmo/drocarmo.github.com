Pedr✿Carm✿

Product Designer & Engineer

Born in the state of São Paulo in Brazil, I’ve spent most of my adult years as an immigrant in the USA — where I studied & developed my skills in technology over the last 15 years. In the present moment, I reside in Miami Beach, Florida with my german shepherd pet, Maya.

For the past 5+ years, I've been designing at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer. There, I lead design for all of Slack Mobile, delivering & guiding innovative product solutions to our 20+ million weekly active users.

Spearheading the design for several of Slack's most successful products including Huddles, an audio & video collaboration tool I co-created, became their fastest growing feature since the launch of Threads. Other notable features I've designed also include: Audio & Video Clips, Emoji Packs, Reacji Solidarity, Widgets & most recently Lists — a new tool for tracking work, managing requests, & coordinating cross-functional projects within Slack's communication flow.

Prior to Slack, I led design in various capacities for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter & Spring. Outside of work, my passions include traveling, reading & photography, particularly using traditional film & digital cameras like the Ricoh GR III & Fujifilm X-T30. I also enjoy playing chess, completing crossword puzzles, running half marathons, playing soccer & exploring the culinary arts through cooking classes.
