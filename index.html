Product Design & Engineer

Born in the state of São Paulo in Brazil, I moved to the US at an early age. Over the last 15 years, I've been living here as an immigrant, studying & developing my skills in technology.

For six years, I've been designing at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer. There, I lead design for Slack Mobile, delivering & guiding innovative product solutions to our millions of users. Prior to Slack, I led design in various capacities for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter & Teespring.
