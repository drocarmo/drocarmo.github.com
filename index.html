Pedro Carmo

A multi-disciplinary designer born in Brazil, living in Miami, FL.

Pedro has spent the last 4+ years working at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer.

He’s led mobile design for features like Huddles; a successful audio-focused calling product, which became Slack’s fastest growing feature since the launch of Threads. Other notable products he’s designed include Audio & Video Clips, Emoji Packs & currently Lists; Slack’s brand new capability to track work, triage requests, and manage cross-functional projects in the flow of communication.

Before Slack, he led design for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter & Teespring. Outside of work, Pedro’s hobbies include traveling, reading, photography with traditional film, Ricoh GR III or Fujifilm X-T30, creating art collages, running half marathons, taking culinary classes & learning Français avec Duolingo.
