about work

Hi, I'm Pedro Carmo.

I'm a designer and front-end developer.

I began my journey in design after attending a Design Thinking Workshop held by a friend of mine, Robb. I began pursuing design by learning how to write HTML/CSS. I also began taking private Illustrator/Photoshop lessons through friends in the Graphic Design industry. Not long after I decided to drop college and attended a school in Chicago (StarterLeague), to learn and continue my practice in Visual Design and Front-end Development.

I now live in Downtown Chicago, and lead design at a development shop called Codal. I also occassionally mentor students at StarterLeague and take part organizing a free monthly event called CreativeMornings in Chicago.

If you wanna work on something, or have something for me to work on. Don't hesitate.
