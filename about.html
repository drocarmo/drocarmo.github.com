about writing

Hi, I'm Pedro Carmo.

I'm a designer and front-end developer.

I began my journey in design after attending a Design Thinking Workshop held by a friend of mine, Robb. Not long after that I attended a school in Chicago (StarterLeague), to learn and practice Visual Design and Front-end Development.

I now live in Downtown Chicago, and lead design at a development shop called CMScentral. I also occassionally mentor students at StarterLeague and take part organizing a free monthly event called CreativeMornings in Chicago.

If you wanna work on something, or have something for me to work on. Don't hesitate.
