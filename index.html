Pedro Carmo

Designer, photographer and digital artist born in the great state of São Paulo, Brasil.

Residing in the sandy streets of Miami FL, Pedro spends his days working at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer. He leads mobile design for the Virtual Headquarters team across the iOS, Android and iPad platforms. Some notable product features he's designed include Huddles, Audio & Video Clips, Emoji Packs and more in the past 3+ years.

Before Slack, he led design for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter and Teespring. For more details, here’s his full résumé. For his design portfolio, please reach out below in the contact section.

Outside of work, Pedro’s hobbies include traveling, reading, photography, creating art collages, playing chess, running half marathons, taking culinary classes and learning new languages.
