I'm Pedro Carmo.

A product designer living in Chicago, Illinois.

I design and build software for people. I like to approach design in a holistic manner while always regarding people over technology.

I'm currently working as lead designer at DevMynd. I co-organize a home for Chicago Product Designers and volunteer monthly at CreativeMornings. Outside of design, you can find me dodging the potholes of Lakeview with my skateboard.

On the internet, I'm actively on Twitter, Github, Tumblr and Rdio. Some of my work is scattered on Dribbble and Behance. Go nuts.
