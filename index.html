I'm Pedro Carmo.

A digital product designer living in Chicago, Illinois.

Designing and building the web is what I love doing. I was fortunate enough to have mentors teach me to always seek the why of problems, not only the what. So, when designing, I strive to find the perfect medium in design to communicate a product to the user. My rule of thumb in design is: design is about how a product works, not just about how it looks.

During my evenings, I mentor design students at a school called StarterLeague. I took a few courses there some time ago, it always feels good to give back to the community what it gave to you. On some friday mornings, I take part in organizing a monthly breakfast event called CreativeMornings, I'm usually the guy giving out way too many high fives.

On spare time, I enjoy pretending that I'm a professional photographer, I'm always trying to up my collection game on Rdio and am probably the worst person at finishing books, but I've managed to read a few.

If you want to be catch me on the web, you can find me actively on Twitter, Quora, Github, or Dribbble.

I'm always taking on projects or passing them along to talented friends. So if you wanna work on something, or have something for me to work on, don't hesitate.
