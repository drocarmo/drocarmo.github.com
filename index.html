Hello, my name is Pedro Carmo.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer based in foggy San Francisco, California.

Over the past 2 years, I’ve been a product designer at Teespring designing a new way for people shop and create custom products. Beyond Teespring, I also mentor design students at Designlab.

Before San Francisco, I was a product designer at Devmynd, rolling up my sleeves in the consultancy world in Chicago, Illinois. Apart from Devmynd, I also took part in building some amazing design communities at CreativeMornings Chicago and The Starter League.
