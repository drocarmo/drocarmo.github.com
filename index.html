Pedr✿Carm✿

Product Designer & Engineer

Born in São Paulo, Brazil but I’ve spent most of my adult years as an immigrant in the USA — where I studied & developed my skills in technology as a designer. Currently, I reside in Miami Beach, Florida with my dog Maya.

For the past 5+ years, I've has been designing at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer. There, I lead the Core Mobile design organization to deliver & guide innovative product solutions to their 20+ million weekly active users.

Spearheading the design for several of Slack's most successful products such as Huddles — which became their fastest growing feature since the launch of Threads. Other notable features I've contributed to designing include: Audio & Video Clips, Emoji Packs, Reacji Solidarity, Widgets & most recently Lists — a new tool for tracking work, managing requests, & coordinating cross-functional projects within Slack's communication flow.

Prior to Slack, I led design in various capacities for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter & Spring. Outside of work, my passions include traveling, reading & photography, particularly using traditional film & digital cameras like the Ricoh GR III & Fujifilm X-T30. I also enjoy playing chess, completing crossword puzzles, running half marathons, playing soccer & exploring the culinary arts through cooking classes.
