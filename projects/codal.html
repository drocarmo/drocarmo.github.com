Project: Codal

Description: Previously known as CMSCentral, now Codal, I had the task to re-designed the brand and consequently, the website of this Chicago development studio. I joined a team made up of myself, one front-end developer, one UX designer and one marketing strategist. I created all of the assets, including typography choice, color palettes, layout and grid. I also wrote most of the HTML/CSS/Javascript including all responsive design.

Tools: Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, SCSS/CSS & Javascript
