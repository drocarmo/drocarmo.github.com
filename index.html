Hi, I'm Pedro Carmo.

I'm a digital designer living in Chicago, Illinois.

I work as lead Product Designer at a development studio by the name of Codal. Whether building an application, re-designing a website or crafting a logo, I'm building a digital product. Most importantly, a product people will use and interact with. Understanding that interaction is why I'm passionate about design and why I build products.

During my evenings, I mentor design students at a school called StarterLeague. I took a few courses there some time ago, it always feels good to give back to the community what it gave to you. On some friday mornings, I take part in organizing a monthly breakfast event called CreativeMornings, it's usually the guy giving out way too many high fives.

On spare time, I enjoy pretending that I'm a professional photographer, I'm always trying to up my collection game on Rdio and am probably the worst person at finishing books, but I've managed to read a few.

I'm always taking on projects or passing them along to talented friends. So if you wanna work on something, or have something for me to work on, don't hesitate.
