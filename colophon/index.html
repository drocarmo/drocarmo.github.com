Colophon


Designed and built by myself, at a coffee shop, somewhere. All of the code is public, and can be seen or used here. Just make sure to give credit where it's due if you want to use anything (that goes for rest of the internet too, rookie).

Built in HTML, Sass and Javascript, generated and maintained with Jekyll. Typefaces include Rooney Web and Rooney Sans by Jan Fromm, and Minion Pro by Robert Slimbach.

A special thanks to Greg Williams for all of the hand in the Ruby + Jekyll. Also, thanks to some friends who inspire me daily: Robb, Mig and Kyle+Kim, all of the StarterLeague family and all of CreativeMornings Chicago team.
