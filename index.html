Pedro Carmo

Designer, artist & periodic engineer from the great state of São Paulo, Brasil.

Residing in the sandy streets of Miami FL, Pedro spends his days working at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer. He leads mobile design for the Virtual Headquarters team across the iOS, Android & iPad platforms. Some notable product features he's designed include Huddles, Audio & Video Clips, Emoji Packs & more in the past 3+ years.

Before Slack, he led design for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter & Teespring. For more details, here’s his résumé. For his design portfolio, please reach out below in the contact section.

Outside of work, Pedro’s hobbies include traveling, reading, shooting photos with his Ricoh GR III or Fujifilm X-T30, creating art collages, playing chess, running half marathons, taking culinary classes & learning new languages.
