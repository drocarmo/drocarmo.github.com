Pedro Carmo

Product Designer & Engineer

Born in São Paulo, Brazil but spent most of his adult years as an immigrant in the USA — where he studied & developed his skills in technology as a designer. Currently, he resides in Miami Beach, Florida.

For the past 5 years, Pedro has been designing at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer. He leads the Core Mobile design organization to deliver & guide innovative product solutions to their 20+ million weekly active users.

He’s spearheaded the design for several of Slack's most successful products such as Huddles — which became their fastest growing feature since the launch of Threads. Other notable features he's designed include: Audio & Video Clips, Emoji Packs, Reacji Solidarity, Widgets & most recently Lists — a new tool for tracking work, managing requests, & coordinating cross-functional projects within Slack's communication flow.

Prior to his work at Slack, he led design in various capacities for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter & Teespring. Outside of work, Pedro’s passions include traveling, reading & photography, particularly using traditional film & digital cameras like the Ricoh GR III & Fujifilm X-T30. He also enjoys playing chess, running half marathons, playing soccer & exploring the culinary arts through cooking classes.
