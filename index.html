Pedro Carmo

A multi-disciplinary designer born in São Paulo, now residing in Miami Beach.

Pedro has spent the last 5+ years working at Slack Technologies as Principal Product Designer, leading the Mobile Product Design organization.

He’s led design for many of Slack's successful products like Huddles — which became their fastest growing feature since the launch of Threads. Other notable features he’s designed include Audio & Video Clips, Emoji Packs, Widgets & most recently Lists — Slack’s brand new capability to track work, triage requests, and manage cross-functional projects in the flow of communication.

Before Slack, he led design for other technology companies such as Envoy, Simple Bank, Kickstarter & Teespring. Outside of work, Pedro’s hobbies include traveling, reading, photography with traditional film, Ricoh GR III or Fujifilm X-T30, creating art collages, running half marathons, playing soccer & taking culinary classes.
