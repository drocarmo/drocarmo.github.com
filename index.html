I'm Pedro Carmo.

A product designer living in Chicago, Illinois.

Design is about people, their needs crossing paths with technology, cognitive science and a synthesis of beauty. Luckily, I get to solve those needs with the talented crew at DevMynd.

If you want to be catch me on the web, you can find me actively on Twitter, Quora and Github.
